## 🌟 About The Project

**Campus Market** is a student-focused marketplace platform where students can buy, sell, exchange, and share educational resources and products.

Students can upload:

📚 Notes

📖 Books

💻 Laptops

📱 Mobiles

🎧 Accessories

📄 Study Materials

and connect directly with buyers and sellers.
